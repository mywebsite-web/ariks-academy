About Ariks International Academy

Located at 17 Alarere Layout, off New Ife Road, Ibadan, Ariks International Academy accepts students of all backgrounds and provides a strong foundation for future academic success. Our staff are highly dedicated and committed to quality education and guidance.

Academics & Curriculum

Curriculum

We offer a blend of Nigerian and British curricula, with a Montessori approach in the early years to promote independence and hands-on learning.

Teaching Approach

Interactive teaching techniques, small-class mentorship, strong values-based education, and regular assessments to monitor progress.

Co-curricular Activities

  • STEM club & science fairs
  • Arts, music & drama
  • Inter-house sports & debates

Assessment & Progress

Regular term assessments, parent-teacher meetings, and continuous feedback ensure every child progresses.

Admissions

How to Apply

  1. Download & fill the admission form (or get from school office).
  2. Submit form with required documents and passport photos.
  3. Attend the screening/test & parent interview where applicable.
  4. Receive admission offer and complete fee payment to secure place.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • Passport photos
  • Previous school reports (where applicable)
